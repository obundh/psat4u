# PSAT4U Distribution Notice

PSAT4U can be distributed for free as an offline Android APK when built from the current app source.

## Distribution Conditions

- The app is provided as an unofficial learning tool.
- Do not describe it as an official PSAT app or an app endorsed by any exam administrator.
- Do not bundle official past exam questions, commercial workbook content, paid lecture material, scraped explanations, or copyrighted passages unless you have confirmed redistribution rights.
- Keep the third-party open source notices with the release materials.
- Re-check Android permissions before each public release.

## Recommended Store Or APK Description

PSAT4U is a free, unofficial, offline-first study app for practicing Korean Public Service Aptitude Test style skills. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by any exam administrator. Training questions are generated locally from app-owned templates and are not official past exam questions.
