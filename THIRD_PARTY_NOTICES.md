# PSAT4U Third-Party Notices

PSAT4U is a free, unofficial, offline-first study app for practicing Korean Public Service Aptitude Test style skills. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any exam administrator, public agency, trademark holder, publisher, or test-prep company.

The app does not bundle official past exam questions, commercial workbook questions, scraped explanations, or third-party problem text. Current drills are generated locally from app-owned templates and random values.

## Offline And Privacy Notice

PSAT4U stores study records, wrong-answer notes, timing stats, and preferences in local device storage. The app does not require an account, server, analytics endpoint, advertising SDK, or remote API for its training features.

For a fully offline build, do not add remote fonts, remote images, analytics, crash reporters, ads, or API-backed question generation unless the privacy notice and app permissions are reviewed again.
